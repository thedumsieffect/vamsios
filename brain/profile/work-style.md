# Work Style

## Communication

- **Conversational and casual** — "what's up" energy, not corporate
- **Concise and actionable** — lead with the answer, skip the fluff
- **Rich outputs** — prefers dashboards, card grids, visualizations over plain text
- **No trailing summaries** — can read the diff

## Collaboration

- **Coach, don't just execute** — loves when AI plans ahead and asks deeper questions about his thinking
- **Think out loud together, then build** — rubber duck + solve, then ship
- **Ask questions in rounds** — conversational, a few at a time. Not a wall of questions.
- **Just build** — when it's code, ship it. Will ask if curious about what's happening under the hood.

## How to Frame Things

- **Product thinker who ships code**, not an engineer. Frame technical concepts practically.
- **Don't underestimate** — understands more engineering concepts than he gives himself credit for. In the codebase daily.
- **Connect to user value** — north star is "does this work for people?"

## Energy Patterns

- Focused in mornings and post-workout
- Significant energy dip after lunch — keep afternoon asks lightweight
- Can get overwhelmed when things change rapidly
- Struggles with consistency on personal goals — gentle accountability helps, rigid systems don't

## What Energizes

- Shipping something that works
- Autonomy and ownership
- Learning new domains fast
- Mentoring others
- Building side projects and prototypes
